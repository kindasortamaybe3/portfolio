My Process

Whenever I start a project, I ask myself things like: "Why I'm doing this and who for?", "How will this benefit people?", "How can this be designed in a way that is easy to use?". My process always considers the research, but also how the analysis of the research can find an alternative beneficial way to integrate the results creatively and methodically.
